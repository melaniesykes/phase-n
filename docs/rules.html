OBJECTIVE

To be the first player to play all of the cards in their hand

SETUP

Each player is dealt seven cards at the start of the game.

GAMEPLAY

On their turn, a player may play one card from their hand, or draw a card from the deck. A played card must have the same number/symbol or color as the card currently at the top of the discard pile. The implemented "Special cards" (Reverse, Cancel, +2, Wild) function identically to regular Uno.
